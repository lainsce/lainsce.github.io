Development Philosophy

Software should feel like an extension of thought, not an obstacle to it. I'm interested in the emotional resonance between person and interface. Tools can understand and respond to human rhythms rather than forcing adaptation to rigid systems. Working with Fyra Labs, I'm exploring interfaces that breathe with their users, moving beyond traditional user-centric design toward something more intimate and intuitive.
